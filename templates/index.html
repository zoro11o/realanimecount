RealAnimeCount

Stop counting every sequel separately.
RealAnimeCount recalculates your true anime total by grouping all related seasons, sequels, side stories, and spin-offs into unified franchise clusters.

Unlike AniList, which counts every season as a separate entry, this tool merges connected anime using relation graph traversal (DFS) and calculates accurate lifetime watch time — including partial watches and rewatches.
